Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How far in advance should I book a photography session?

For weddings and major events, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your preferred date. Portrait sessions and smaller events can typically be scheduled 2-4 weeks ahead. However, we always try to accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

What should I wear for my photo session?

We recommend wearing something you feel confident and comfortable in. Solid colors and simple patterns work best. Avoid busy patterns, logos, or anything too trendy. We'll provide a detailed style guide once you book, tailored to your specific session type and location.

How long does it take to receive my photos?

Portrait sessions: 1-2 weeks. Wedding photography: 4-6 weeks. Commercial projects: 2-3 weeks. All photos are professionally edited and color-corrected. You'll receive a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share your images.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We understand that professional photography is an investment. We offer flexible payment plans for wedding and large event packages. Typically, we require a 30% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance due before or on the day of the shoot.

What happens if it rains on the day of my outdoor session?

We monitor weather forecasts closely. Light rain can actually create beautiful, moody photos! For heavy rain, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost or move to an indoor location. We always have backup plans to ensure your session is perfect.

Can I request specific shots or poses?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share inspiration photos and specific shot ideas. We'll review your requests during our pre-session consultation and incorporate them into our shooting plan while also using our expertise to capture unexpected, candid moments.

Do you travel for destination weddings or events?

Yes! We love destination shoots and have photographed events across the country and internationally. Travel fees vary depending on location and duration. Contact us with your destination details for a custom quote.

Will my photos be edited and retouched?

All delivered photos include professional editing: color correction, exposure adjustments, and artistic enhancement. Basic retouching (removing blemishes, flyaway hair) is included. Advanced retouching is available as an add-on service for an additional fee.
